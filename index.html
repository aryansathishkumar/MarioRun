AI MARIO GAME









Source code: Linuk // game credit: Mario Run

Instructions


Move your right hand to the right according to the camera to move the mario right

Move your right hand to the left according to the camera to move the mario left

Move your right hand to the top according to the camera to make the mario jump

Jump at the right time to tackle your enemies

There are 4 lives. So you could play 4 turns and get as many points as you can

Wishing you the luck!
